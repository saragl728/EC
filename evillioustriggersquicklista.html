Intro

This is to contain a list of the dark subjects, triggers and squicks that can be found in the Evillious Chronicles novels in release order.

Some specific triggers/squicks will have a internal link to a context.

If after reading this, you think it's too dark for you, then don't read the novels.

I know that Evillious also has its lighter moments, but after reading this you shouldn't sell it as something fluffy. It isn't.

If you want to read the novels, but don't know Japanese, you can check pricechecktranslations in tumblr. There's also translations into Spanish.
